# 0004: Home And Discovery Model

Status: `accepted`
Date: `2026-04-22`

## Decision

Home prioritizes `Continue Watching` first. Discovery remains explicit, finite, and intentional rather than engagement-driven.

## Reasoning

- The product is for returning to archived content, not maximizing watch time.
- Continue Watching is the highest-value shortcut for the primary use case.
- An intentional information architecture better fits family and curated-library scenarios.
